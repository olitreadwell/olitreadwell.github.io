While I was writing today I came across this piece of inner monologue.

<blockquote>The thing about memories, is that I don't create my own memories. I haven't been going out and finding fun things to do. I haven't really figured out how to do that. It's super hard for me to do...."</blockquote>

### I wonder why I talk to myself the way I do.

It's easy for me to continue talking down to myself, to tell myself something is hard, to say that I can't do something.

If I do I end up not doing well at that thing. Giving up before I begin.

Nothing I want to do is easy, and no not even taking the first step will be easy, none of the steps will be easy. Even the simplest of hard things seems to be more difficult than it needs to be.

There's so many easy things out there that you can fall into the trap of doing. Taking a walk to buy food when I'm angry is the easy thing to do. Coming home and going on imgur is the easy thing to do. Getting distracted by twitter or yelp or tinder or okcupid is the easy thing to do. Telling yourself that things are hard is the easy thing to do. Doing the things that need to be done is the hard thing to do.

### Doing things that are important to you is hard.

Restricting your food, eating healthy, counting calories, working out, making plans and keeping to them, calling your friends and family, not drinking, not smoking.These all seem hard, but after you start doing them they don't feel so hard anymore. It just becomes easier.
Waking up and coding everyday used to feel hard, but now it doesn't. Goodness, I've been doing it for a full year. Going to the gym every damn day used to feel hard, but it's not anymore. Making time to read used to feel hard, but in the last few months I've read a quite a few more books than I have in the last 3 years.

Writing every day is still hard, coding after work is still hard, asking for help is still hard, playing socialising and being a friend is still hard, giving trust giving love, that's still hard. But life is much easier if you do the hard things, even if its starts as doing it just once.

Can I manage to do these unpleasant things for just 10 more seconds?
